Welcome

Anokha, which translates to ‘UNIQUE’, has emerged as one of the most popular and top engineering techfests in India. Anokha Techfest, with the theme Sci-Fi, was a platform for the best minds in India to showcase their talents, innovations and ingenuity. It aimed at motivating engineering students from different disciplines to come together and share mutual enthusiasm and passion for science and technology.
